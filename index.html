Garuda Wisnu Kencana (GWK)

10 Hours

IDR 950.000

The Garuda Wisnu Kencana (GWK) statue in Bali is a 121-meter tall statue depicting the God Vishnu and the Garuda bird. Located in the GWK Cultural Park, it serves as a tourist icon and a center for Balinese arts and culture, officially inaugurated in 2018!

Uluwatu Temple With Kecak Dance

1 hour

IDR 150.000

Uluwatu Temple, perched on a 70-meter cliff in Bali, offers stunning ocean views and cultural significance. Visitors can enjoy the mesmerizing Kecak dance at sunset, which brings the Ramayana epic to life. This sacred site beautifully blends spirituality, and tradition!

Monkey Forest Ubud

1 - 6 hours

IDR 275.000

The Ubud Monkey Forest, also known as the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary, is a lush haven in Bali, home to playful long-tailed macaques. This spiritual site features ancient temples and diverse flora, offering visitors a unique blend of nature, culture, and wildlife in a serene environment!
